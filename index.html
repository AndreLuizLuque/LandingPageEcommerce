01 FIRST ARC

Adidas in China

Adidas identifies strong growth in the Chinese market and decides to create a product line aimed directly at this audience. The focus is to merge local culture with global fashion and to grow the brand's relevance in the region.

PHASE BEGINNING
02 Initial design

Design development

The Adidas design team in Shanghai leads the project. The visual is based on the traditional Tang style, adapted to an urban format. Cultural elements are reinterpreted in a modern language.

PHASE EMERGENCE
03 Launch

Launch and viral moment

The jacket is released as a limited edition, with restricted distribution mainly in Asia. Its striking look and cultural concept make the product go viral on social media.

PHASE RECOGNITION
04 Journey

Demand and expansion

High demand causes the product to sell out quickly. Scarcity drives up its resale value and reinforces its status as a rare item. Over time, the trend spreads globally.
